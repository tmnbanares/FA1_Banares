Christmas

Christmas Eve and Christmas Day

  • I enjoy the planning we do and the excitement that we feel every Christmas Eve.
  • The best parts of Christmas are the dishes, which taste even better every Christmas season, the bright, flashy decorations, the carolers, and the gifts we get from our loved ones.
  • The nicest feeling ever is having to take a break from school so that you can spend more time with your family and friends.
  • The feeling of gratitude that comes from being reminded of Jesus' birth, and the after-church gathering is always enjoyable and it will always be.
